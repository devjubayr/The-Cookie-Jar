Our Sweet Story: Baked with Passion in Dhaka

From a single home oven to the **Best Homemade Cookie Shop in Dhaka**.

The Start of The Cookie Jar

The Cookie Jar began in 2020 when our founder, Fatima, started baking her grandmother's secret chocolate chip recipe for local friends in **Gulshan, Dhaka**. The overwhelming demand for her rich, moist, and perfectly textured cookies quickly showed that Dhaka craved authentic, **homemade quality**. Today, we maintain that same small-batch philosophy, ensuring every cookie meets our high standard.

Our Commitment to Quality

We source the finest ingredients, from premium Belgian chocolate to local, free-range eggs. We never use preservatives or artificial flavors. This commitment is why customers across **Bangladesh** trust us to deliver not just a cookie, but a genuine moment of comfort and indulgence.
